Research Overview

This research investigates the robustness of neural network-based audio processing systems against adversarial attacks. We evaluated two prominent architectures: ECAPA-TDNN and ResNet, using 16 different target texts to assess their vulnerability to adversarial perturbations while maintaining audio quality.

Key Findings

  • • Comprehensive evaluation across 16 diverse target texts
  • • Comparative analysis between ECAPA-TDNN and ResNet architectures
  • • SNR vs Similarity trade-off analysis for each target
  • • Robustness evaluation across different attack scenarios
